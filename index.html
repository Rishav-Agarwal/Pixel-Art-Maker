How to use?

  • Best viewed on Google Chrome.
  • Select grid size and click `Submit` to create the pixel grid.
  • Choose color from color picker.
  • Paint on the pixel grid my left click or drag.
  • Right click or drag to erase.
  • Click `Reset` to reset the pixel grid.
  • Click `Preview` to preview the gird.
  • Click `Download` to download as png.
  • To import the pixel grid as html file or text, click `Import`.
  • Click `Export` to export the pixel table as html.
